# BRIEF.md — Exercise Brief

## Epilogue

This project was built as part of a ~3 hour home assignment for the **GenAI Exploration Lead — Junior / Graduate** role at Zap Group.

The goal of the assignment was not to be tested under pressure, but to demonstrate:

- A structured approach to breaking down a real business problem
- Thinking about data — what to extract, from where, and how
- Meaningful use of AI tools to solve a real-world automation challenge

The solution is a working prototype, not a production system. Design decisions and production extensions are documented in `README.md` and `PLAN.md`.
